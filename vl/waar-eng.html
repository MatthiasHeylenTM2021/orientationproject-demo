Where can you find the foundling hatch?

The foundling hatch (B) is easily accessible by public transportation.

From the central station (A), you have to walk about 15 minutes.

You can also use a streetcar or bus, which depart from the Koningin Astridplein.

The streetcar and bus stop where you have to get off is called "Drink".

  1. Bus lines 23, 4xx
  2. Tramline 24

Here are directions:

  1. Get off at the Kerkstraat stop.
  2. Walk down Church Street and take the fourth side street to the right, Groenstraat
  3. vervolgens de eerste straat links, de Helmstraat
  4. The foundling hatch is located on the left side in number 93

For help and information, you can always contact our emergency phone:

0478 / 81 31 28
