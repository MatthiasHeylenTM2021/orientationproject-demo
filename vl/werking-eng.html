Operation

The opaque glass street door labeled "foundling slide" is open day and night.

The foundling hatch is behind that door.

The mom is alone and not visible to others. What she does and how long she stays there is unprecedented.

Pull open the hatch.

Place your child in the crib and cover warmly.

Please bring the cover with license plate number and related information. You will find our contact information, an explanation of what will happen to your child and a registration number that confirms you as the person who left the child behind. You can always contact us anonymously. You can return to your decision during 6 months.

When the hatch is opened, the silent alarm is activated. Within 15 minutes, a staff member will be with your child.

A doctor will be notified immediately. He or she will examine your child. If necessary, your child will be taken to the nearest hospital.

Letter and puzzle piece

Inside on the flap of the hatch is an envelope with the invitation "Take Along", The intention is that the mom takes the envelope with her and can look at it later in peace.

In the cover are a letter and a puzzle piece.

In the letter, the mom reads about what will happen to her child in the future, and the crisis phone is clearly reported once again.

The puzzle piece is half of a whimsical card cut into 2. The other piece stays with us. If we find a child in the hatch and the cover is gone, we note the details of the found child on our piece.

The piece in the cover is a link to the child. We invite the mother to keep the piece. There may come a time when she wants to give information to her child. The link piece helps with that.
