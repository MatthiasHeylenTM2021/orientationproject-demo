VL - 0478/ 81 31 28

Findings are not a new, but fortunately a very rare phenomenon.

Sometimes newborns are abandoned in an unsafe place so they rarely survive.

The foundling hatch is there to give a distraught mother the chance to anonymously give her baby a warm and safe home.

In imitation of many other countries, the VZW Moeders voor Moeders established the first foundlings' hatch in Belgium in 2000. It is located in the Helmstraat 93 in Borgerhout, Antwerp

The foundling hatch has its own 0478813128 that can be reached 24/7. This way, the mother can contact it anonymously for a chat or for useful advice and action.

VL info

The vl is an initiative of the non-profit organization mothers for mothers.
